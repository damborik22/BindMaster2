# Choosing the Right Binding Assay

## Decision Matrix

| Designs | Budget | Assay | KD Range | Time |
|---------|--------|-------|----------|------|
| > 100 | Any | NanoBiT first pass | Qualitative | 1-2 days |
| 20-100 | > $5k | BLI single-conc screen | 1 nM - 100 uM | 2-3 days |
| 10-50 | > $10k | BLI kinetics | 1 nM - 100 uM | 3-5 days |
| < 10 | > $15k | SPR full kinetics | pM - uM | 1-2 weeks |
| Leads | Any | SPR + ITC + DSF | Full thermo | 2-4 weeks |

## Recommended Workflow
1. Screen all with BLI single-concentration (1 uM)
2. Dose-response on hits (response > 0.1 nm)
3. Full kinetics on top 3-5 with SPR
4. Characterize best 1-2: DSF + SEC-MALS

## Cost Estimates (Academic)
- Gene synthesis: ~$100-200/gene
- BLI campaign (20 designs): ~$500-2000
- SPR campaign (5 leads): ~$1000-5000
- Total 20-design campaign: ~$3000-8000
